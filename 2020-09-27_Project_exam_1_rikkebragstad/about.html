The story behind SpaceX

SpaceX is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transportation services company that was founded by a man named Elon Musk in 2002. His goal was to reduce space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars. This idea came to life after conceptualizing Mars Oasis in 2001, a project that was going to land a miniature experimental greenhouse on Mars that was going to grow plants.

He said that “This would be the furthest that life’s ever traveled. Since then SpaceX has developed several launch vehicles, such as the Starlink satellite constellation, and the Dragon cargo spacecraft.

“You want to wake up in the morning and think the future is going to be great - and that’s what being a spacefaring civilization is all about. It’s about believing in the future and thinking that the future will be better than the past. And I can’t think of anything more exciting than going out there and being among the stars.”

- Elon Musk

SpaceX today

Major achievements of SpaceX are in the reuse of orbital-class launch vehicles and cost reduction in the space launch industry. Most notable of these being the continued landings and relaunches of spacecrafts.

In March 2018, SpaceX had over 100 launches, and as of today SpaceX is the leading global commercial launch provider measured by manifested launches, and the first private company to send astronauts to the International Space Station.
