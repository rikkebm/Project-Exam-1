Our goal

Space and everything happening outside of our safe planet Earth can sometimes feel overwhelming and confusing, and that is exactly why SpaceX has created a place where kids can explore and get familiar with what space travel, and everything that comes with it, is all about.

SpaceX Kids wants to make children aware that even though there are still a lot in space that is yet to be explored, and that is unknown for us, space is not something that you need to fear. In fact, space can be exciting. It gives us uportuneties beyond our planet, and gives us somthing to wonder about, dream about, and it gives us hope for a lot more in the future than we know today.

>So either if you need some info for your homework or there is an astronaut growing inside of you, SpaceX Kids is a place for you to find the answers to your questions. We are still working on expanding or website, but until then, why don't you send us a message and ask us directly?
